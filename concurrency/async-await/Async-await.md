## Async Await

#### Concurrency
- The ability to execute more than one program or task simultaneously 

#### Swift pre-async/await 
- Grand Central Dispatch (DispatchQueues)
- Completion Handlers `(...) {result in}` to notify us when task is done
- Combine Framework
